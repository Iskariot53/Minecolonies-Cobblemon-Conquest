Changes


- Added Bosses: Entei, Raikou, Suicune, Lugia and Ho-oh which can spawn in 3v1, 2v1, and 1v1 formats. Bosses cannot be captured, but if defeated in battle, will grant the player the defeated cobblemon as a reward.
- Bosses have a minimum level of 50, max level of 200 and scale with current party. Every 10 minutes there is a 20% for a boss to be selected and spawn.
- Fixed mega and apricorn bee outputs.
