Changes


- Additional fixes for issues with stewmelier
- Additional recipe unification tweaks.
- Disabled project resonance guide book given on world join as it is a part of the tome already given.
- Disabled ballast advancements toast/announcement. 
- Picked up with further progress on Oritech and Eternal Starlight questlines, both will be an on-going W.I.P over future updates.
- Battle tower cobbledollars distribution nerfed approx 50%, rewards updated - master/ancient origin balls now cost 50bp per.
- Battle tower recipe updated.
- Since people were already doing it in preparation while not knowing if it was even posible.. Added compatibility to use fluids as aeronautic ship fuel.
	- Standard Fuels. All "bio" fuels and ethanols = 16k(tick) burn rate. Biodiesel = 20k. Turbofuel = 24k. Gasoline = 26k. Diesel = 30k.
	- High Energy Fuels. Liquid D-T Fuel = 70k (tick)
- Misc. small tweaks and fixes to hopefully resolve some isolated crash issues (parsing enchantments, etc.)
- Removed the ability to make steel via shaped crafting entirely, as well as tweaked a few early-game oritech recipes to require metals other than steel until it can be properly crafted at a point and in a way that makes sense, without needing another mod to make it.
